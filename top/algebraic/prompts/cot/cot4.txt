Q: 86 - 92 + 32 + 43 - 31 + 37 + 36 - 88
A: Let's think step by step. Subtract, 86 - 92 = -6. Add, -6 + 32 = 26. Add, 26 + 43 = 69. Subtract, 69 - 31 = 38. Add, 38 + 37 = 75. Add, 75 + 36 = 111. Subtract, 111 - 88 = 23. So the answer is 23.
