Q: 86 - 92 + 32 + 43 - 31 + 37 + 36 - 88
A: Let's think step by step. Subtract, 86 - 92 = -6. Add, -6 + 32 = 26. Add, 26 + 43 = 69. Subtract, 69 - 31 = 38. Add, 38 + 37 = 75. Add, 75 + 36 = 111. Subtract, 111 - 88 = 23. So the answer is 23.

Q: - 57 - 47 + 94 + 58 - 14 + 35 + 79 + 93
A: Let's think step by step. Subtract, -57 - 47 = -104. Add, -104 + 94 = -10. Add, -10 + 58 = 48. Subtract, 48 - 14 = 34. Add, 34 + 35 = 69. Add, 69 + 79 = 148. Add, 148 + 93 = 241. So the answer is 241.
